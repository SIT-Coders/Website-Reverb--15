About Reverb

Reverb is the annual cultural extravaganza organized by Symbiosis Institute of Technology. Reverb, short for Reverberation, is the undue prolongation of sound in a particular space after the original sound is removed. Just like its definition above, Reverb is the Numero Uno place to be, whether it be the jaw-dropping dance sequences, the head banging action of the Battle of the Bands, or the electrifying performances at Pronite, rest assured, you’ll be transported into an unforgettable journey where encores are the norm, and your presence will never fade away… Come to Reverb… Be Heard!

ABOUT SIT

Symbiosis Institute of Technology is one of the youngest members of the Symbiosis International University. A part of Symbiosis Knowledge Village, Lavale. SIT is located amidst the lush green hills in the city of Pune. The college imparts equality B.Tech course to over 1600 students in 5 different branches : Civil, Computer Science, Electronics and Telecommunication, Information Technology and Mechanical. We have recently ventured into a new domain and SIT now offers P.G and M.Sc. courses in various fields.


In the cultural domain, students at SIT are active participants in in-house self-development programmes like Performing Arts & Crafts and Liberal Arts, which finds a place in the overall curriculum of the institute. The impact of such activities is evident through the participation and accolades won by our students in various national-level academic and extra-curricular competitions like Oasis (BITS, Pilani), Sympulse (SCMS-UG, Pune), Aakriti (IIT Bombay), Mood Indigo (IIT Bombay) and many others.
